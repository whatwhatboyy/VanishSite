Refund Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Here's our refund policy.

Effective Date: December 27, 2024

Quick Summary

  • Refund Window: 14 days from purchase date
  • Eligibility: Product must not function as described
  • Processing Time: 5-10 business days after approval
  • Refund Method: Original payment method

About Our Products

We sell digitally delivered software products and services related to video games. Our offerings include custom-developed software tools, utilities, and configuration files designed to enhance user experience.

All products are delivered electronically after purchase. Customers must already own the original games that our products are designed to work with.

1Refund Eligibility

We offer refunds under the following circumstances:

  • Technical Issues: The product does not function as described on the product page
  • Delivery Failure: You did not receive the digital product after purchase
  • Duplicate Purchase: You accidentally purchased the same product twice
  • Compatibility Issues: The product is incompatible with your system despite meeting stated requirements

All refund requests must be submitted within 14 days of the original purchase date.

2Refund Request Process

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

1

Contact Support

Email us at refunds@vanishunleashed.com with your order number and reason for the refund request.

2

Provide Details

Include your order confirmation, purchase date, and a detailed description of the issue you're experiencing.

3

Review Process

Our team will review your request within 2-3 business days and may request additional information.

4

Refund Processing

If approved, refunds are processed within 5-10 business days to your original payment method.

3Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be issued in the following situations:

  • Request made more than 14 days after purchase
  • Change of mind or no longer wanting the product
  • Issues caused by third-party software, antivirus, or firewall settings
  • Failure to follow installation instructions or product documentation
  • Using the product in violation of our Terms of Service
  • Account suspension or termination due to policy violations
  • Products that have been successfully used or accessed
  • Issues arising from not owning the original game the product is designed for

Important Notice

Due to the digital nature of our products, once a product has been downloaded and accessed, we may not be able to offer a full refund. We encourage you to contact our support team before making a purchase if you have any questions about product compatibility or functionality.

4Technical Support Before Refund

Before processing a refund request, our support team will attempt to resolve any technical issues you may be experiencing. This may include:

  • Providing updated installation instructions
  • Troubleshooting compatibility issues
  • Offering alternative solutions or product replacements
  • Providing guidance on system configuration

Many issues can be resolved with proper support, saving you time and ensuring you get the most value from your purchase.

5Partial Refunds

In some cases, we may offer partial refunds:

  • Subscription Products: Prorated refund for unused subscription time
  • Bundle Purchases: Partial refund if only some products in a bundle are affected
  • Extended Usage: Reduced refund amount if product was used extensively before the issue was reported

6Refund Method

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Refund credited to the card used (5-10 business days)
  • PayPal: Refund sent to PayPal account (3-5 business days)
  • Other Methods: Refund issued via original payment provider

Please note that your bank or payment provider may have their own processing times that are beyond our control.

7Chargebacks

We encourage customers to contact us directly before initiating a chargeback with their bank or credit card company. Chargebacks result in additional fees and may delay the resolution of your issue.

If you file a chargeback before contacting us:

  • Your account may be suspended pending investigation
  • Access to purchased products may be revoked
  • Future purchases may be restricted

We are committed to resolving issues fairly and promptly when contacted directly.

8Consumer Rights

This refund policy does not affect any statutory rights you may have under applicable consumer protection laws. If you are located in the European Union, you may have additional rights under EU consumer law.

For digital content, please note that by completing your purchase and accessing the product, you acknowledge that delivery has begun and you may lose your right of withdrawal under EU regulations.

9Policy Changes

We reserve the right to modify this refund policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to our website. The refund policy in effect at the time of your purchase will apply to that transaction.
