Refund Policy

Our commitment to fair and transparent refund practices for all digital products.

⚠

Important: Digital Products

Due to the instant-delivery nature of digital products, all sales are generally final once download access has been provided. However, we evaluate refund requests on a case-by-case basis and are committed to customer satisfaction.

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Overview

At Vanish Unleashed, we stand behind the quality of our digital products. While digital goods are typically non-refundable after delivery, we understand that issues can arise. This policy outlines when refunds may be considered and the process for requesting one.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refund requests are evaluated individually based on the circumstances. Below are general guidelines for eligibility:

✓ May Qualify for Refund

  • Product significantly different from description
  • Critical functionality doesn't work as advertised
  • Technical issue we cannot resolve
  • Duplicate purchase (accidental)
  • Product not delivered within 24 hours

✕ Generally Not Eligible

  • Change of mind after download
  • Incompatibility with your specific setup
  • Failure to read product requirements
  • Issues caused by third-party modifications
  • Requests after 14 days of purchase

3. Refund Timeframe

To be considered for a refund, requests must be submitted within the following timeframes:

  • Scripts & Server Resources: Within 14 days of purchase
  • Graphics Packs & Visual Mods: Within 7 days of purchase
  • Bundles: Within 14 days of purchase

Requests submitted after these periods will generally not be considered unless there are exceptional circumstances.

4. Refund Process

Follow these steps to request a refund:

1

Contact Support

Email billing@vanishunleashed.com with your order number, purchase date, and reason for the refund request.

2

Provide Documentation

Include any relevant screenshots, error messages, or evidence supporting your refund request.

3

Allow Troubleshooting

For technical issues, we may ask to troubleshoot the problem first. Many issues can be resolved with support assistance.

4

Review & Decision

We'll review your request within 3-5 business days and respond with our decision. We're a small team, so please be patient.

5

Refund Processing

If approved, refunds are processed to your original payment method within 5-10 business days.

5. Partial Refunds

In some cases, we may offer partial refunds. This includes:

  • Bundle purchases where only some products have issues
  • Products that partially work as described
  • Situations where significant support time was provided

6. Non-Refundable Items

The following are generally non-refundable:

  • Custom development work or commissioned scripts
  • Installation services once completed
  • Products where you've violated our Terms of Service
  • Products that have been resold or redistributed

7. Chargebacks and Payment Disputes

IMPORTANT: You MUST contact us before filing any chargeback or payment dispute.

We understand that issues can arise, and we are committed to resolving them fairly. However, chargebacks for digital products you have received cause significant harm to our business and are taken very seriously.

Before Filing a Chargeback:

  • Email billing@vanishunleashed.com with your order number and issue
  • Allow 1-3 business days for our response (we're a small team)
  • We will work with you to resolve any legitimate issue

Consequences of Filing a Chargeback Without Contacting Us:

  • Immediate and permanent revocation of all purchased product licenses
  • Permanent ban from future purchases
  • We will dispute the chargeback with full documentation including:
    • Proof of delivery (email confirmation, download logs)
    • IP address and access records
    • Acceptance of terms at checkout
    • Any product usage/access logs
  • Your information may be reported to fraud prevention databases
  • We reserve the right to pursue legal action for fraudulent chargebacks

What Constitutes a Fraudulent Chargeback:

  • Claiming you didn't receive a product that was delivered to your email
  • Claiming "unauthorized transaction" when you made the purchase
  • Claiming the product was "not as described" without first contacting support
  • Filing a dispute after downloading and using the product

We maintain comprehensive records of all transactions and deliveries. These records will be used to dispute any illegitimate chargeback claims. Our refund policy is fair and reasonable - there is no valid reason to file a chargeback without first contacting our support team.

8. Contact Information

For refund requests or questions about this policy:

  • Email: billing@vanishunleashed.com
  • Discord: Join our server for faster response
  • Response Time: 1-3 business days (we're a small team)

Need Help with a Refund?

Contact our team and we'll work with you to resolve any issues. We're committed to your satisfaction.
