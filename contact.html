Frequently Asked Questions

How do I receive my purchase?

All products are delivered electronically. After purchase, you'll receive an email with download links and installation instructions.

What if my product isn't working?

Contact our technical support team. We'll help troubleshoot the issue or provide a refund if we can't resolve it within our policy terms.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer refunds within 14 days of purchase if the product doesn't work as described. See our refund policy for full details.

How long do updates last?

Update duration varies by product. Check the product description for specific update policies and duration.

Is my payment secure?

Yes, all payments are processed through secure, PCI-compliant payment processors like Stripe and PayPal.

Do I need to own the game?

Yes, customers must already own legitimate copies of any games that our products are designed to work with.
