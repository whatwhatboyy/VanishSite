Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about joining our team

How much do editors get paid?

Pay varies based on project complexity and your experience level. On average, editors earn £15-50 per video depending on length and requirements. Top performers can earn significantly more.

How does the partner program work?

Partners receive discounted rates (up to 25% off), priority turnaround, and a dedicated editor. You can also earn commission by referring other creators to our services.

Is this a full-time or part-time position?

Our editor positions are freelance-based. You can work as much or as little as you want. Take on projects when you're available and decline when you're busy.

What software do I need?

We primarily work with Adobe Premiere Pro, DaVinci Resolve, and Final Cut Pro. Having After Effects is a plus for motion graphics work.

How long does the application process take?

We typically review applications within 48 hours. If selected, you'll receive a test project to complete. The entire process usually takes about a week.
