Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

What is your turnaround time?

Standard turnaround is 3-5 business days depending on project complexity. Rush delivery (24-48 hours) is available for an additional fee.

How many revisions are included?

All projects include 2 rounds of revisions. Additional revisions can be purchased if needed. We work with you until you're satisfied.

How does your preview system work?

We send you a watermarked preview of your completed video before you pay. You can review it, request revisions, and only pay once you're completely satisfied. The watermark is removed after payment.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal through our secure payment processor. Payment is collected only after you approve the watermarked preview.

Can I make changes to the video myself?

Yes! After reviewing the watermarked preview, you can request specific changes or we can send you the project files (for an additional fee) so you can make edits yourself.

How do I send my footage?

After payment, we'll provide upload links via Google Drive, Dropbox, or WeTransfer. We can accommodate files of any size.
