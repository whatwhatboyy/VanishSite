Refund Policy

Our clear and fair policy for digital product refunds

Last Updated: December 2024

Digital Product Refund Policy

Due to the digital nature of our products and instant delivery, all sales are generally considered final once the product has been delivered. Digital products cannot be "returned" in the traditional sense, as access is granted immediately upon successful payment.

Refund Eligibility

We may consider refund requests under the following circumstances:

  • Non-Delivery: If you did not receive the product or access credentials after successful payment
  • Technical Issues: If the product is technically unusable on your system and our support team cannot resolve the issue
  • Duplicate Purchase: If you accidentally purchased the same product twice

Refund Request Process

To request a refund:

  • Contact our support team at support@vanishunleashed.com
  • Include your order number and the email address used for purchase
  • Provide a detailed description of the issue
  • Include any relevant screenshots or error messages

Timeframe

Refund requests must be submitted within 14 days of the original purchase date.

Processing

Approved refunds are processed through the original payment method (Stripe or PayPal). Processing typically takes 5-10 business days.

Non-Refundable Situations

  • Change of mind after product delivery
  • Failure to read product descriptions before purchase
  • Issues caused by third-party software updates
  • Violation of our Terms of Service
