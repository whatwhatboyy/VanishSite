Support FAQ

What's the fastest way to get help?

Discord is usually our fastest channel. Community members can often help quickly, and we respond when we're available — usually within a few hours.

Why might responses be delayed?

We're a small team that occasionally travels. We do our best to respond quickly, but please allow 1-3 business days for email and be patient on Discord.

When is live chat available?

Live chat availability varies based on our schedule. Check Discord for our current status, or leave a message and we'll get back to you.

What if my script isn't working?

Post in Discord with your server framework (ESX/QBCore), error messages, and server.cfg. The community or our team will help when available.

Do you offer installation services?

Yes, we offer paid installation services. Keep in mind scheduling depends on our availability, so please plan ahead if you need this service.

Can I request custom scripts?

Yes, we accept custom projects when our schedule allows. Reach out via email or Discord, but please note turnaround times will depend on our workload.

About Our Support

Vanish Unleashed is a small, independent team of passionate FiveM developers. While we can't promise instant responses, we're committed to helping every customer succeed. We appreciate your patience and understanding — quality support is always worth the wait!
